Pistols

Wingman Single-Fire

The Wingman can be enhanced with the Boosted Loader Hop-Up, which reduces the weapon's reload time if a reload is performed while the mag is close to being fully depleted and adds 2 rounds to the mag upon reloading.

Sub Machine Guns

R-99 Full Auto

The R-99 SMG is a sub machine gun that utilizes light rounds and has the fastest TTK against any weapons in Apex Legends

Assault Rifle

R-301 Full Auto

The R-301 Carbine is an assault rifle that utilizes Light Rounds and one of the easier guns in the game. It is the most popular pick among players in Apex Legends making it always on S-Tier.

Senapan Serbu

Flatline Full Auto

The Flatline is an assault rifle that utilizes Heavy Rounds and one of the harder guns in the game. It is a popular pick among seasoned players in Apex Legends which means that this gun take more skill than the others.

Senjata Mesin

Alternator Full Auto

The Alternator is a Care Package Weapon that utilizes Light Rounds and one of the easier guns in the game. The Disruptor Rounds makes it bullets deals twice the damage when hitting Shields.
